Republic of Benin

The Republic of Benin is a West African nation, formerly known as Dahomey, bordering Togo, Nigeria, Burkina Faso, and Niger. It has a long coastline on the Bight of Benin, part of the Gulf of Guinea. The country's official language is French, but many other languages are spoken, including Fon and Yoruba.
