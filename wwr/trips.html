Featured Trips

Big Mallard Rapids

Difficulty: Intermediate

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Navigate through the challenging Big Mallard Rapids, featuring Class III and IV rapids. This trip offers stunning canyon views and thrilling whitewater action perfect for experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline rush.

Starting at $149 per person

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Advanced

Duration: 2 Days

Conquer the legendary Los Dientes del Diablo rapids, known for their intense Class IV and V difficulty. This overnight adventure includes camping under the stars and navigating some of the most challenging whitewater in the region.

Starting at $299 per person

Half Day Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner

Duration: 4 Hours

Perfect for first-time rafters and families. This gentle introduction to whitewater rafting features Class I and II rapids, beautiful scenery, and plenty of time to learn basic rafting techniques from our expert guides.

Starting at $89 per person
