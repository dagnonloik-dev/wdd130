Rafting Website description

A rafting website aims to provide information and facilitate bookings for whitewater rafting experiences. It serves as a platform to educate potential customers about rafting, showcase available trips, and allow for easy online reservations.

History

The history of rafting websites is intertwined with the broader history of whitewater rafting, which evolved from early exploration and transportation to a recreational sport. Initially, rubber rafts were developed for military use in the mid-19th century, with wider recreational adoption taking off in the 1970s. Rafting websites today offer a variety of information, from trip planning and safety guidelines to historical accounts of the sport.
